Instructions All reports are given as .csv files that can be opened in Excel, Google Sheets, Numbers, or as a text file.

Order Report - Gives a report of all orders in a specified timeframe. This includes all order information.
On Hand Quantities - Gives a report of all items currently on hand and their quantities.
Part Usage by Retailer - Gives a report of all items ordered by retailer in a specified timeframe.
By Date Part Usage - Gives a report of how many times an item has been ordered in a specified timeframe.
